The Science

Eight steps from
brain to insight

Every second, NEURA runs a complete pipeline from raw neuron signals to a focus score on your screen.

01 —
🎧

Wear the Headband

Dry Ag/AgCl electrodes at Fp1, Fp2, and reference positions make contact with the forehead. No gel needed.

02 —
⚡

EEG Acquisition

ADS1299 samples at 256 Hz, capturing microvolt-level neural oscillations across Alpha, Beta, and Theta bands.

03 —
📡

BLE Transmission

ESP32 transmits 20-byte packets every 50ms via BLE GATT Notify (UUID 0xFFE1) to your phone. No internet needed.

04 —
🔍

Signal Filtering

Band-pass 0.5–40 Hz removes drift and muscle noise. Notch filter at 50 Hz eliminates powerline interference.

05 —
📊

FFT Analysis

256-point Hanning-windowed FFT extracts Alpha (8–12 Hz), Beta (12–30 Hz), and Theta (4–8 Hz) power spectral density.

06 —
🤖

ML Classification

Hybrid Random Forest + Logistic Regression model classifies cognitive state: Focused, Moderate, Distracted, or Relaxed.

07 —
🎯

Focus Score

Score = β ÷ (α + θ) × 100. Updated every 500ms. Compared against your personal baseline for relative accuracy.

08 —
🔔

Smart Feedback

If focus drops below threshold: haptic vibration + adaptive soundscape triggers. No screen interruption.
